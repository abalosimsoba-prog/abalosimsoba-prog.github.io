Provided tutorial instruction in inferential statistics and optimization to first-year Master's students enrolled in the Big Data program. Focused on guiding students through theoretical foundations and applied problem sets to develop strong statistical reasoning.

### Inferential Statistics Modules
* Hypothesis Testing
* Confidence Intervals
* Parameter Estimation
* Likelihood-Based Inference
* Sampling Distributions
* Asymptotic Results

### Optimization Modules
* Unconstrained Optimization
* Constrained Optimization
* Lagrange Multipliers
* KKT Conditions
* Gradient-Based Optimization Methods
* Convex Optimization Fundamentals

### Key Responsibilities
* Delivered clear and structured tutorials on core mathematical concepts.
* Guided students step-by-step through complex theoretical foundations.
* Supported learners in developing effective optimization-based solution strategies.
* Facilitated the transition from theoretical statistics to big data applications.
